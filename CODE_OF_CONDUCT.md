# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org) Code of Conduct v2.1.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and inclusive.
- Accept constructive criticism.
- Focus on what is best for the community.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at `jan.neuhaus@vaya-consulting.de`.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.

The maintainers are committed to enforcing this Code of Conduct.
